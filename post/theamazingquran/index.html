A CASE FOR GOD

WHAT TO EXPECT IN THE QURAN (This may worth your time …)

The atheist proves 'there is no god'.

The agnostic corrects 'but God'.

The Quran challenges 'there is no god but GOD'.

Many worship one God, and in some cases along with other gods.

The rest of us, 'to hell with god'. Then we call upon Him with such deep sincerity in moments when there is none else to call upon.
